Top 5 Tips to Improve Your Aim

Practicing aim doesn't have to be hard. Here are the best exercises used by pro players that you can start today.

  1. Use aim trainers like Aim Lab or Kovaak’s FPS Aim Trainer.
  2. Practice tracking moving targets daily.
  3. Adjust your mouse sensitivity to find what feels natural.
  4. Warm up before every gaming session with 10-15 minutes of drills.
  5. Focus on posture and hand positioning to maintain consistency.
