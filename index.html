Popular Foods Around the World

Pizza (Italy)

Pizza is one of the most famous foods worldwide. Originating from Naples, Italy, it is known for its round crust, tomato sauce, cheese, and variety of toppings.

Why it is famous: Easy to share, delicious, and with endless flavor combinations. It became popular worldwide thanks to its simplicity and universal taste.

Basic Pizza Recipe:

  • Dough: 500 g flour, 300 ml water, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp sugar, 7 g dry yeast
  • Sauce: Crushed tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, salt, and oregano
  • Toppings: Mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, mushrooms, peppers (optional)

Preparation: Mix flour, water, salt, sugar, and yeast. Let rest 1 hour. Roll out dough, add sauce, cheese, and toppings. Bake at 220°C for 12–15 minutes.

Where to get ingredients: Local supermarkets, Italian stores, or international food markets.

Drinks to pair: Italian red wine, beer, soft drinks, or mineral water.

Sushi (Japan)

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish combining vinegared rice with raw fish, seafood, or vegetables. It is famous for its artistic presentation and delicate flavor.

Why it is famous: Healthy, light, and combines unique flavors with attractive presentation. Popular worldwide, especially in Japanese restaurants.

Basic Sushi Rolls Recipe (Makis):

  • Sushi rice: 2 cups, washed and cooked with rice vinegar
  • Nori (seaweed sheets)
  • Fillings: Salmon, avocado, cucumber, tuna (optional)
  • Soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger to serve

Preparation: Place a nori sheet on the rolling mat, spread the rice, add fillings in the center, and roll tightly. Cut into pieces and serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and ginger.

Where to get ingredients: Asian stores, supermarkets with international section.

Drinks to pair: Green tea, Japanese sake, Japanese beer.

Burger (United States)

The burger is an icon of American fast food, known for juicy meat, bread, cheese, and various toppings.

Why it is famous: Quick to prepare, very tasty, and customizable with many ingredients. It is a symbol of fast food and American pop culture.

Basic Recipe:

  • Ground beef: 200 g per burger
  • Burger bun
  • Cheddar cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, ketchup, and mustard

Preparation: Shape the beef into patties and cook on a griddle. Assemble the burger with bun, meat, cheese, and vegetables.

Where to get ingredients: Local supermarkets, butcher shops, and bakeries.

Drinks to pair: Soft drinks, beer, fruit shakes.
